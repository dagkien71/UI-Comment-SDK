Overview

UI Comment Extension ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

No Personal Information Collected

Our extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers or any third parties.

Local Data Storage

  • All comments and data are stored locally in your browser's localStorage
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Your data remains completely private and under your control
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the extension

Technical Information

  • Extension usage statistics (if enabled by Chrome)
  • Browser version and extension version
  • No personal identifiers are collected

How We Use Information

Local Functionality

  • Store your comments and annotations locally
  • Enable extension features and functionality
  • Maintain your preferences and settings

No External Processing

  • We do not process your data on external servers
  • We do not analyze your browsing habits
  • We do not track your online activities

Data Security

Local Storage

  • All data is encrypted by your browser's security
  • Data is stored only on your device
  • No network transmission of your data

No Third-Party Access

  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not use your data for advertising

Your Rights

Complete Control

  • You can delete all extension data at any time
  • You can disable the extension to stop all functionality
  • You can export your data for backup purposes

Data Portability

  • All data is stored in standard JSON format
  • You can export your comments and annotations
  • You can import data from other sessions

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
